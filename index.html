Knowledge Base & RAG
Ground Agents in your Data.
A production-grade RAG pipeline spanning 4 phases of iterative improvement. From document ingestion through multi-strategy chunking, hybrid search, reranking, and self-grading — with 20+ configurable retrieval feature flags.
  • ✓Document Ingestion — PDF (with Markdown preprocessing), DOCX, TXT, Markdown, Excel — auto-processed into searchable chunks
  • ✓Multi-Strategy Chunking — Recursive, structure-aware, semantic, and sentence strategies with configurable target tokens
  • ✓Hybrid Search — Combines dense vector and keyword-based search with intelligent fusion for superior retrieval accuracy
  • ✓Reranking Pipeline — Pluggable reranking with multiple providers to push the most relevant results to the top
  • ✓Adaptive Query Routing — LLM classifies query complexity and auto-decomposes into parallel sub-queries
  • ✓Self-Grading — Post-generation quality check with annotation queue flagging for low-quality responses

Chain Human Input Nodes
